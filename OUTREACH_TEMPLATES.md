## Tips for Using These Templates

### ✅ DO:
- Customize each message with specific details
- Mention specific projects or skills
- Keep it concise and professional
- Include direct links to portfolio
- Proofread before sending
- Follow up after 1 week if no response

### ❌ DON'T:
- Send identical messages to everyone
- Make it too long (keep under 250 words)
- Be too casual or informal
- Forget to include contact information
- Leave broken links
- Send at odd hours

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines That Work:
- ✅ "CS Student | Your ML Engineer Opening at [Company]"
- ✅ "Interested in [Role] Position at [Company]"
- ✅ "Let's Connect - Full-Stack Developer"
- ❌ "Hi" or "Job Application"

### Response Rates:
- Cold email: ~2-5% response rate (industry average)
- Referral: ~30-40% response rate
- LinkedIn message: ~10-15% response rate
- Keep following up respectfully

---

## Success Metrics

Track these to improve your outreach:

1. **Open Rate**: How many emails are opened
2. **Response Rate**: How many people reply
3. **Meeting Rate**: How many lead to conversations
4. **Offer Rate**: How many lead to opportunities

**Goal**: Get 3-5 informational interviews/month

---

## Final Tips

1. **Personalize Everything**: Reference specific projects or company details
2. **Be Authentic**: Show genuine interest, not just desperation for a job
3. **Show Value First**: Mention what you can contribute, not just what you want
4. **Be Professional**: Check grammar, spelling, and tone
5. **Follow Up**: Most opportunities require 2-3 touches
6. **Stay Positive**: Rejection is part of the process

---

**Remember**: Your portfolio is your strongest asset. Make sure every link works 
and showcases your best work! 💪

Good luck with your outreach! 🚀
