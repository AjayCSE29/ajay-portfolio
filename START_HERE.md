## ⚡ Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### 1️⃣ Open Your Portfolio Right Now
- Double-click `index.html` 
- Your browser will open your website!
- Test it out - click around!

### 2️⃣ Update Your Basic Info (5 minutes)
Edit these in `index.html`:

**Find and change:**
```html
<!-- Line ~30: Your name -->
<a href="#home">Ajay<span class="accent">.</span></a>
→ Change "Ajay" to YOUR NAME

<!-- Line ~170: Your email -->
<a href="mailto:ajay@example.com">ajay@example.com</a>
→ Change to YOUR EMAIL

<!-- Line ~340: Your city/country -->
<p>Your City, Country</p>
→ Change to YOUR LOCATION
```

### 3️⃣ Update Your Projects (5 minutes)
Find the "Featured Projects" section and update:
- Your project title
- Your project description
- Your GitHub link
- Replace tech tags with your tech

### 4️⃣ Test Everything
- Toggle dark mode (moon icon)
- Resize browser to test mobile
- Click navigation links
- Try the contact form
- Test all links
